FAQs

How do I earn stamps? +

Earn stamps by making qualifying purchases of ¬£20 or more. Each stamp brings you closer to unlocking rewards. You can also earn stamps through special member events and promotions.

What rewards can I unlock? +

As a UO Rewards member, you can unlock exclusive discounts, early access to sales, member-only events, birthday rewards, and special perks throughout the year.

Is membership free? +

Yes! UO Rewards membership is completely free. Simply sign up with your email address to start earning stamps and unlocking rewards.

How do I track my stamp progress? +

Once you're a member, you can view your stamp progress in your dashboard. You'll see how many stamps you've earned and how many you need to unlock your next reward.

Do stamps expire? +

Stamps don't expire, so you can take your time earning them. However, some rewards and offers may have expiration dates, so be sure to check your dashboard regularly.

Can I use rewards online and in-store? +

Yes! Your rewards can be used both online and in-store. When shopping online, your rewards will be automatically applied at checkout. In-store, simply show your membership details at the register.
